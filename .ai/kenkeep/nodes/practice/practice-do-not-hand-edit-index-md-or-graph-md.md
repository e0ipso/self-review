`INDEX.md` (token-budgeted summary injected into every new AI session) and `GRAPH.md` (full edge listing) are regenerated by the lint-staged pre-commit hook whenever nodes change, and staged into the same commit.

Edit nodes under `nodes/`, not these aggregate files. Manual edits will be overwritten on the next commit that touches a node.
